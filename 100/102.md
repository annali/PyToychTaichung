### 人工智慧分類

**Strong AI：**

能夠像人類一樣具備心靈

**Weak AI：**

能夠表現出類似人類思考、推論等等智慧的行為

**4 school of aspect：**

|  | **Human-like** | **Rational** |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Think** | Think humanly | Think rationally |
| **Act** | Act humanly | Act rationally |
